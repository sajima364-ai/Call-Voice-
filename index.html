Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 14 June 2026

Thank you for choosing to use Voice Dost ("we", "us", "our", or "App"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our App collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Access and Use

To provide the core functionality of announcing the caller's name, Voice Dost requires access to certain device permissions. All data processing happens locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or share your data on any external servers.

  • Read Contacts: We access your contacts strictly to match an incoming phone number with the saved name in your phonebook. Your contact list is never uploaded to the internet.
  • Read Phone State: We require this permission to detect when your phone is ringing, answered, or disconnected.

2. Data Security and Storage

Your privacy is our top priority. Voice Dost operates 100% offline. We do not have any servers, and we do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data, phone numbers, or contact details. Everything stays securely inside your phone.

3. Third-Party Services

Currently, Voice Dost does not use any third-party tracking tools, analytics, or advertisement networks. Your usage remains completely private.
